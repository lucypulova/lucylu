Hi, I'm Lucy!
I am an 18-year-old girl who is ready to conquer the world. Having new experiences, meeting new people, and learning new things charge me up. Creating value for people and impacting the world through my work makes me feel gratified. Challenges that enable me to grow are much enjoyable to me. I like to find application in what I am taught and learn as quickly as possible.
During the past 3-years, I have participated in several international projects. Meanwhile, I organized and directed one of my own. Through these vivid experiences, I formed my worldview and gained insight into the various aspects of the culture of many countries. Having lived all around and having met many different people, I learned to adapt to any surrounding or circumstance. Partly due to spending so much time abroad, I have gained excellent command of English. Besides that, I have an excellent command of Russian and Bulgarian.
At present, I am Software & Hardware Science student. Creating visual and software solutions is my passion. I have experience working with HTML, CSS, Javascript, C++, C#, Adobe Creative Cloud, and CorelDRAW Graphic Suite for 5-years and have created over 50 public and personal projects. Currently, I am learning PHP and Ruby and expanding my knowledge in JavaScript.
Presently, I am exploring my interests, pinpointing my best attributes, and gaining new experiences. I aim to make the most of my time.
